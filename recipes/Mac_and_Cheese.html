Mac and Cheese


Mac and cheese

This dish constitutes of macaroni and cheese. The dish is full of rich ingredients which make our brain think it's won the lottery. It is an easy dish to prepare at home and there's so many ways to prepare it. Let us look at one of the ways.


Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings (Salt, pepper, paprika)
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the macaroni, drain and transfer to a prepared baking dish
  2. Mix equal quantities of the flour and butter in a skillet or saucepan and stir constantly over medium-low heat to obtain a roux. The roux should be light and puffy.
  3. Mix the roux with cheese to obtain a cheese sauce, pour it over the macaroni and stir.
  4. Spread some bread crumbs to cover the mixture and sprinkle with paprika
  5. Bake the mac and cheese until the topping is golden brown
