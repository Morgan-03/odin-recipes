Banana Bread

This is an easy recipe to carry out at home. The banana bread is moist and full of flavour. The bread can be served as a snack or dessert after meals. It can also be served with hot coffee, cocoa or tea.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 cups mashed bananas(preferrably overripe bananas)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended.
  3. Banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Serve with hot tea, coffee, cocoa or juice.
